What is
VIVA?

V.I.V.A. is a wearable device that helps athletes prevent hamstring injuries through real-time monitoring and early risk detection. Using EMG and IMU sensors, it tracks muscle activity, fatigue and movement, sending instant alerts and personalised insights to a mobile app. Athletes simply pair the device, complete a baseline test and train as normal while V.I.V.A. monitors risk and provides recommendations.
